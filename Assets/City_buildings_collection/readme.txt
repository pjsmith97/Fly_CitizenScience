This package contains 13 different types of big city-buildings.
Building 01 comes with 8 different texture sets.
Building 02 comes with 4 different variable texture sets per floor.

Just drag the prefabs into your scene and arrange them as you like.

You may also want to have a look at the example scene that contains all the prefabs.

All single parts using about 900-4200 Polygons.

The original FBX-files are also included in the package.
